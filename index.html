STARTING STORIES

Leaving his financial career abroad, Nguyen Tien Ba returned to Vietnam to set up business with the desire to bring practical values to the community.

At that time, Vietnam's fitness market was still quite primitive, with many shortages and misinformation. In order to change this situation, Tien Ba started as Youtube PRIME FITNESS sharing his knowledge of the gym he learned abroad from the world's top coaches.

In order to improve his knowledge, he continued to study in the United States and become the first Vietnamese to receive the PICP certificate of Poliquin Group.

In mid-2018, when Tien Ba decided to establish the first gym PRIME Ultimate Fitness (SUF) in Hanoi, through a continuous development process, the SUF has expanded with the SUF. 2 in Ho Chi Minh City.
